A vector space, also called a linear space, is a collection(set) of [[Vector|vectors]].

Inside this collection, vectors can be manipulated under strict rules through
addition and multiplication(scaling).

Scaling is done by multiplying vectors and real numbers([[Scalar|scalars]]).
